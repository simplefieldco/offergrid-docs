## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [offergrid.io](https://offergrid.io) and register as a reseller.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get verified">
    Complete reseller verification to access the full service catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Generate a Team API Key to access the catalog and place orders via API.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the catalog">
    Search and filter services by category, price, location, and features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place your first order">
    Submit an order with customer details and start earning commissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

### 1. Browse Service Offers

Search the catalog by:
- **Category**: Internet, TV, security, energy, insurance
- **Price Range**: Filter by monthly cost
- **Location**: Check availability by ZIP code
- **Features**: Search by speeds, channels, coverage

### 2. Compare Options

Evaluate offers based on:
- Pricing and fees
- Service specifications
- Provider reputation
- Customer reviews
- Availability in your markets

### 3. Place Orders

Submit orders with:
- Customer contact information
- Service address
- Selected services
- Special notes or preferences

### 4. Track Fulfillment

Monitor order progress:
- Provider acceptance
- Scheduling and installation
- Service activation
- Completion and payment

## Commission Structure

Earn revenue through:

1. **Referral Fees**: Per-order commission for successful activations
2. **Recurring Revenue**: Monthly percentage on active subscriptions (where available)
3. **Volume Bonuses**: Increased rates for high-volume partners
4. **Exclusive Deals**: Special pricing from preferred provider partners

<Tip>
  Commission structures vary by provider and service type. Check individual offers for specific terms.
</Tip>
