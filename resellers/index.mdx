## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [offergrid.io](https://offergrid.io) and register as a reseller.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get verified">
    Complete reseller verification to access the full service catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Generate a Team API Key to access the catalog and place orders via API.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the catalog">
    Search and filter services by category, price, location, and features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place your first order">
    Submit an order with customer details and start earning commissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

### 1. Browse Service Offers

Search the catalog by category, price range, and service address. See exactly what's available at any location—internet, TV, security, energy, and insurance services from multiple providers.

### 2. Compare Options

Evaluate offers side-by-side based on pricing, service specifications, provider reputation, and availability in your markets. Find the best fit for each customer.

### 3. Place Orders

Submit orders with customer contact information, service address, selected services, and any special notes or preferences. Orders are automatically routed to the right provider.

### 4. Track Fulfillment

Monitor order progress from provider acceptance through scheduling, installation, service activation, and completion. Stay informed every step of the way.

## Service Categories

Available services:

- **Internet**: Broadband, fiber, cable, DSL, wireless
- **Television**: Cable TV, satellite, streaming bundles
- **Security**: Home security, monitoring, smart home
- **Energy**: Electricity, natural gas, solar
- **Insurance**: Home, auto, life, health insurance
- **Other**: Additional service categories

## Commission Structure

Earn revenue through:

1. **Referral Fees**: Per-order commission for successful activations
2. **Recurring Revenue**: Monthly percentage on active subscriptions (where available)
3. **Volume Bonuses**: Increased rates for high-volume partners
4. **Exclusive Deals**: Special pricing from preferred provider partners

<Tip>
  Commission structures vary by provider and service type. Check individual offers for specific terms.
</Tip>
